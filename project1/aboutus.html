"If it made of wood, we can built it."
Henry's Woodworking has been in business for over 30 years. When we started we built with mainly oak, but as time went on we started to work with pine and then we added logs for the up north look. As our customer base grew, we expanded into others. From cabinets to caskets, large and small items. For every room in your home. We can help you design your own unique piece.
